Facility-to-Chip™ Cooling Ecosystem

The Borealis™ CDU Facility-to-Chip™ Cooling Ecosystem addresses the escalating thermal, efficiency, and scalability demands of high-density AI/HPC deployments by linking the data center’s facility-level cooling infrastructure directly to the chip-level heat removal hardware. This end-to-end integration enables precise thermal management, maximized efficiency, and simplified scalability — all from a single intelligent platform.

  • Facility Side:Chillers, fluid coolers, and mechanical refrigeration assets form the primary loop.
  • Core Intelligence: The Borealis™ CDU serves as the “brain” of the system, dynamically managing both the facility and chip loops, optimizing thermal envelopes, and integrating AI-driven predictive maintenance.
  • Chip Side: ThermaPod™ modules provide rack-level immersion, spray, or direct-to-chip cooling tailored to workload density.
This architecture delivers closed-loop coordination, enabling real-time optimization across the entire cooling chain.

High-Efficiency Cooling

Borealis™CDU provides efficient cooling tailored for power-dense computing environments, ensuring optimal performance and reliability. It reduces energy consumption compared to traditional cooling methods, making it an eco-friendly solution.

Dual-Loop Flexibility

Unlike conventional CDUs, Borealis™CDU supports both single-phase glycol loops and two-phase dielectric refrigerant loops in the same chassis. Switching between modes requires only a heat exchanger and control logic swap — not a full hardware replacement. This flexibility eliminates the need for parallel infrastructure and future-proof facilities for emerging two-phase deployments.

Modular & Scalable

Our modular design allows for easy integration and scalability, adapting to your specific needs. The flexible operation modes support both single-phase and two-phase cooling, ensuring optimal thermal management for various applications.

Telemetry & Control

Borealis™ CDU integrates advanced telemetry and intelligent control at the row scale, delivering fast transient stability during workload spikes and real-time observability across both facility and IT cooling loops. Operators gain actionable insights through unified dashboards and APIs, enabling predictive maintenance, proactive anomaly detection, and seamless orchestration with data center infrastructure management (DCIM) systems.

High-Efficiency Pumping

Industry-leading motor efficiency (>92%), built-in VFDs, and peer-to-peer load sharing for up to six units without an external PLC. This design: 1. Maintains operation near the best efficiency point (BEP) for minimal parasitic load 2. Reduces system complexity by removing the need for separate staging controllers 3. Increases reliability with fewer failure points

Future-Ready

Borealis™ CUD provides a clean migration path from today’s single-phase glycol systems to tomorrow’s two-phase dielectric solutions — without the need for parallel infrastructure or disruptive retrofits.
